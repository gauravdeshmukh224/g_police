Procedure to Apply

  1. Register yourself on Goa Police Web Portal and login.
  2. Go to Citizen Services Section and select Police Clearance Certificate.
  3. Fill in the application form carefully.
  4. Attach relevant documents and proceed to payment section.
  5. Submit the application and note the reference number for tracking.
